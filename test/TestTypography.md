# Test Typography

filter certain character sequences for easier reading.  
This is sometimes called "smartypants" or just "smart formatting".  

The following character sequences should be converted to output-specific glyphs :

- copyright  ` (c) `  render as  (c)
- registered   ` (r) ` render as  (r)
- trademark    ` (tm) ` render as (tm)
- service mark ` (sm) ` render as   (sm)
- ellipsis     ` ... `   or   ellipsis      ` . . . `  render as  ... and . . .
- em-dash ` --- ` render as  ---
- en-dash   ` -- ` render as --
- one-quarter    ` 1/4 `  render as  1/4  and  ` 1/4th ` render as  1/4th
- three-quarters    ` 3/4 `   or   ` 3/4th `  or   ` 3/4ths ` render as   3/4  ,   3/4th  ,   3/4ths
- one-half    ` 1/2 ` render as 1/2
- ` "double" `  render as  "double"  and  ` 'single' ` is 'single'
